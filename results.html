Results

This section presents the outcomes of our spatial analysis, focusing on pollutant distribution and human exposure.

1. Average Pollutant Map

This section displays spatial representations of average pollutant concentrations across the Czech Republic. The maps are derived from CAMS data and highlight regional variations in air quality over the study period. These visualizations help identify pollution hotspots and patterns over time.

2. Population Exposure Assessment

This part evaluates how pollutant concentrations intersect with population distribution. Using WorldPop data, we estimated how many people are exposed to pollutant levels above WHO thresholds, providing insights into environmental justice and public health risks.
